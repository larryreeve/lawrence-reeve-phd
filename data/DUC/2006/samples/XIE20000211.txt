<HEADLINE> (Urgent) Sinn Fein Says Has New N.Ireland Arms Deal Plan </HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
LONDON, February 11 (Xinhua) -- Sinn Fein, the political wing
of the Irish Republican Army, announced Friday it has submitted a
new proposal on disarmament that could avoid Britain taking back
direct rule over Northern Ireland.
</P>
<P>
A Sinn Fein statement said it was a "breakthrough proposition."
</P>
